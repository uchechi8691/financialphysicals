Leadership

Chioma Amadi-Mgbenka

Founder & Lead Epidemiologist

Chioma Amadi-Mgbenka is an Epidemiologist by training with a background and experience in Informatics. She is passionate about generating evidence to inform patient outcomes. This led her to pursue related professional and academic endeavors over the last decade.

Originating from a resource-poor background, where vulnerable populations faced challenges accessing essential medicines and public health knowledge, she has dedicated her professional and academic pursuits to research and development in global settings.

Her overarching goal is to emerge as a well-rounded innovator committed to enhancing patient outcomes, particularly through real-world insights, which she believes are a gateway to innovation.
